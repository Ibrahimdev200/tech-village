About Tech Village

Tech Village, a premier tech institution under Lerafin Education Hub, was established with the vision of empowering the next generation of tech innovators. Our journey began in 2019, and we officially launched in June 2024. We are dedicated to providing high-quality tech education and practical skills training to students, helping them to thrive in the rapidly evolving tech industry. At Tech Village, we offer a range of comprehensive training programs, including web development, data analysis, graphics design, cybersecurity, and more. Our curriculum is designed to be hands-on and industry-relevant, ensuring that our students are well-prepared to meet the demands of the tech world..

Mission

At Tech Village, our mission is to empower individuals through cutting-edge technological education and training. We aim to foster a culture of innovation, creativity, and excellence, preparing our students to excel in the tech industry and make significant contributions to their communities.

Vision

Our vision is to become a leading tech training institution, recognized globally for our commitment to excellence in education and our ability to produce highly skilled, industry-ready professionals. We aim to create a community of tech enthusiasts who are passionate about learning, innovation, and making a positive impact on society through technology.

Join us at Tech Village and be a part of a dynamic and supportive learning environment where innovation thrives and futures are built.
