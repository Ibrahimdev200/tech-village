Apply to Tech Village

Congratulations!

Your form has been submitted successfully and is currently being reviewed. One of our admission officers will contact you within 48 hours regarding the next steps for admission and the oral interview.

Please note that the application process is free. However, you are required to pay an acceptance fee of 5,000 Naira within two weeks of receiving your admission letter.

The training fees for each skill are as follows:

  • All skills: 60,000 Naira
  • Computer Training and Maintenance: 40,000 Naira

These fees can be paid in two installments for your convenience: 50% at the start of the session and 50% on or before the second month on campus.

Thank you for choosing Tech Village!
